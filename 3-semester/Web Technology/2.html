Patient Check-up Process

The following are the processes that a patient will follow when they enter for a complete check-up:

  1. Patient registration
    • Patient arrives at the registration desk
    • Patient presents their ID and insurance card
    • Patient fills out a registration form
    • Patient pays their copay
  2. Medical history and examination
    • Patient meets with a nurse to take their vital signs and medical history
    • Patient meets with a doctor for a physical exam
    • Doctor may order additional tests, such as blood work or X-rays
  3. Diagnosis and treatment
    • Doctor reviews test results and diagnoses the patient
    • Doctor discusses treatment options with the patient
    • Patient receives treatment
  4. Follow-up and discharge
    • Patient may need to schedule follow-up appointments
    • Patient receives discharge instructions
    • Patient leaves the hospital
